Introduction
SpeAna is the simplest and minimal spectrum analyzer Mac app that is the best companion with Oscillo. You can discover synths in a traditional way with backup of the latest technology. It spends significantly low cpu%, so you can keep watching it without a worry:)

Usage
Select your desired audio input device and channel, you'll watch frequency vs level graph. Leftward- lower frequency, rightward- higher frequency, and upward- higher level. You can typically use it to watch how a filter sweeps. Hit Pause button (or space bar) to pause/resume SpeAna.

A little more usage
You can use it while mastering your music. SpeAna should look somehow like the screenshot which looks like the town of L.A. on the left half, and Pacific Ocean on the right half;)
